About AMAMI Japanese Language School

Message from the Director

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to AMAMI Japanese Language School. Our mission is to help you achieve your dreams of studying in Japan by providing you with the highest quality language education and comprehensive support throughout the visa application process.

At AMAMI, we understand that embarking on a journey to study abroad can be both exciting and daunting. That's why we are committed to making your transition as smooth as possible. Our experienced and dedicated staff are here to guide you every step of the way, from the moment you decide to pursue your studies in Japan to your arrival and beyond.

Our curriculum is designed to cater to students of all proficiency levels, ensuring that you not only learn the language but also immerse yourself in the rich cultural tapestry of Japan. We believe that language learning is most effective when it is engaging and relevant, which is why our courses are interactive and tailored to meet your individual needs and goals.

Moreover, we pride ourselves on our supportive community. At AMAMI, you will not only gain linguistic skills but also build lasting friendships and connections that will enrich your experience in Japan. Our diverse student body and inclusive environment foster a sense of belonging and mutual respect, making AMAMI a home away from home.

We also offer a range of extracurricular activities and cultural experiences to enhance your learning journey. From traditional tea ceremonies to exploring historical sites, our programs are designed to give you a deeper understanding of Japanese culture and society. We are excited to have you join our vibrant community and look forward to helping you turn your dreams into reality. Together, let's embark on this remarkable adventure.

Director - Mr. Ramesh Dhakal
